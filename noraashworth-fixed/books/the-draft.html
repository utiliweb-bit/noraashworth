About the Author

Nora Ashworth

Nora Ashworth writes about the quiet architecture of families — the protective lies, the letters never sent, the names people carry that were never quite their own. Her novels move between continents and generations, but return again and again to the same question: what do we owe the people who kept a silence for our sake?
