About the Author

Nora Ashworth

Nora Ashworth writes about the quiet architecture of families — the protective lies, the letters never sent, the names people carry that were never quite their own. Her novels move between continents and generations, but return again and again to the same question: what do we owe the people who kept a silence for our sake?

Each book is a complete, standalone story. There are no cliffhangers here — only the slow, careful unfolding of something a family has spent decades not saying, and what it costs, and what it's worth, to finally say it.

The Journey

Nora finished her first full draft — a story about a woman who never learned her mother's native language — late one winter, mostly at a kitchen table.
